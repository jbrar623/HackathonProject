About Us

Try Our Questionare

Turning Point is both a website and an app that provides invaluable resources and support for individuals facing addiction, whether it's a personal struggle or someone you care about. Our mission is to offer a lifeline to those seeking help and recovery, fostering a community of understanding and assistance. We're here to guide you through your journey towards a healthier and happier life, helping you or your loved one take the crucial steps toward recovery and a brighter future.

Use this questionare to find out about you or someone elses addiction severity. You might not know it, but you might actually have a addiction!

Previous Events

Community Support Workshop

Date: October 5, 2023

The Community Support Workshop brought together recovering drug addicts and mental health professionals in Calgary. The event featured interactive sessions focusing on coping strategies, stress management, and building a strong support network. Attendees gained valuable insights, shared personal experiences, and learned effective tools to navigate the challenges of recovery.

Recovery Marathon

Date: September 15, 2023

The Calgary Recovery Marathon drew participants from all walks of life. Recovering drug addicts, their families, and supporters joined forces in a collective spirit of resilience and determination. The event included a marathon, group exercises, and inspirational speeches. Participants celebrated their achievements, fostering a sense of community and reinforcing the importance of a healthy, drug-free lifestyle.

Art Theraphy Exhibition

Date: August 28, 2023

The Art Therapy Exhibition showcased the creative talents of recovering drug addicts in the Calgary area. Through various art forms, participants expressed their emotions, struggles, and triumphs in the journey to sobriety. The exhibition provided a platform for artists in recovery to share their stories with the community, fostering understanding and empathy. Visitors admired the artworks, reinforcing the power of artistic expression in the healing process.
