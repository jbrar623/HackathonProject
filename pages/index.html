About Us:

Turning Point is both a website and an app that provides invaluable resources and support for individuals facing addiction, whether it's a personal struggle or someone you care about. Our mission is to offer a lifeline to those seeking help and recovery, fostering a community of understanding and assistance. Through our user-friendly platform, you can access a wealth of information, connect with experienced professionals, and find inspiration from stories of triumph over addiction. We're here to guide you through your journey towards a healthier and happier life, helping you or your loved one take the crucial steps toward recovery and a brighter future.
